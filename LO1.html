Opdracht

To make a great portfolio, make your portfolio a project (rather than a rush job). As with the other projects you do this semester, your portfolio has an owner (you), a target audience (your teachers). Make use of the techniques and methods for visual design, user experience and development offered in this semester. Make sure that you also take into account the feedback you receive during the portfolio review sessions. Consider, for example, the following activities: Look for inspiration: watch and learn Create a visual identity for yourself Sketch the layout of your pages. Name the different parts: what do you want to show on a page? Design a number of templates for different types of pages. Use as much real content as possible (text and images). Test your designs with the target group Start coding on time
