• Rebranding:

    In this project, you learn to visually represent a brand. you experiment with stylescapes, deliver a brand guide and write a content strategy. For this content strategy you develop marketing means. It is important to create a distinctive identity that fits the brand's mission and vision and is appealing to the target audience. Clients are artists, athletes or start-ups. A precondition is that there are little communication means (logo, corporate identity, photography).

  • Media Campaign

    In this project, you work work on a media campaign. The client indicates what the goal of the campaign should be, e.g. awareness, sales, change in behavior. Your goal is to properly research the intended target group and how to involve them. Then create a campaign that is obviously appropriate to the intended brand and has been well thought out. Clients are existing brands whose identity is fixed. It is important that you not only work out a campaign, but also develop interactive means to support the campaign, e.g. games, a competition or events.
