Soundlab

Opdracht

In this project, you learn to visually represent a brand. you experiment with stylescapes, deliver a brand guide and write a content strategy. For this content strategy you develop marketing means. It is important to create a distinctive identity that fits the brand's mission and vision and is appealing to the target audience. Clients are artists, athletes or start-ups. A precondition is that there are little communication means (logo, corporate identity, photography).
